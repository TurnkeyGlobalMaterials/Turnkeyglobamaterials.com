How We Work

From spec to
delivery — handled.

We work directly with multifamily developers, senior living operators, and commercial builders to spec materials early and coordinate delivery on your construction schedule. No inventory required on your end.

01
Initial Consultation
Share your project scope, unit count, and finish spec targets. We'll identify which categories make the most sense and provide preliminary pricing.
02
Sample & Spec Review
Receive physical samples and detailed spec sheets. We'll coordinate any customization — finish colors, hardware, dimensions — before order placement.
03
Order & Production
Once approved, we place your order directly with the manufacturer. Production and QC typically takes 60–75 days from confirmed PO.
04
Logistics & Delivery
We coordinate customs clearance, freight, and final delivery to your project site or staging warehouse. Typically 90 days door to door.
